Towers of Hanoi

The objective of the game is to move all three blocks from the first base to the third and have them laid out in the same arrangement. But there are a few catches. You can only move one block at a time, every move involves taking one block and setting it on another base, and no disk can be put on top of a smaller disk. You can play by selecting the base to highlight the topmost block you'll need to move and then selecting the base you want to move that block to.
